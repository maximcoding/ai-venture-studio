# Product Backlog

## User Personas
- **Busy Professional**: A working professional (25-45 years old) with a moderate to high disposable income, struggling to balance work-life demands and prioritize healthy eating.
  - Example: Sarah, a marketing manager at a startup.

## User Stories

### High Priority (P0)

1. **Generate Personalized Meal Plans**
	* As a Busy Professional, I want the app to generate meal plans based on my dietary needs and preferences, so that I can save time planning meals.
	* Acceptance Criteria:
		+ The app asks for user's dietary restrictions and preferences
		+ The app generates 3-5 personalized meal plans per week
	* Priority: P0
2. **Customizable Recipe Generation**
	* As a Busy Professional, I want the app to generate recipes based on my cooking skills and ingredient availability, so that I can easily prepare meals.
	* Acceptance Criteria:
		+ The app asks for user's cooking skills and available ingredients
		+ The app generates recipes with detailed instructions and ingredient lists

### Medium Priority (P1)

1. **Grocery List Generation**
	* As a Busy Professional, I want the app to generate grocery lists based on my meal plans, so that I can easily plan and purchase ingredients.
	* Acceptance Criteria:
		+ The app generates a grocery list for each meal plan
		+ The app allows users to modify the grocery list as needed
2. **Social Sharing**
	* As a Busy Professional, I want to share my meal plans and recipes with friends or family members, so that they can also benefit from the app.
	* Acceptance Criteria:
		+ Users can share meal plans and recipes via social media platforms
		+ Users can view shared meal plans and recipes in their feed

### Low Priority (P2)

1. **Integration with Food Delivery Services**
	* As a Busy Professional, I want the app to integrate with food delivery services, so that I can order ingredients directly from the app.
	* Acceptance Criteria:
		+ The app integrates with popular food delivery services
		+ Users can place orders for ingredients within the app

## Dependencies
- User Story 1 (Generate Personalized Meal Plans) depends on User Story 2 (Customizable Recipe Generation)
- User Story 3 (Integration with Food Delivery Services) depends on successful integration of social sharing features
