# Assumptions & Unknowns

**Assumptions.md**

### Unknowns to Verify

* What is the optimal pricing strategy for Invoicely?
* How will we balance user acquisition costs with revenue growth?
* Can we achieve significant market share without sacrificing profitability?

### Dependencies (External Factors)

* Market demand for invoicing software: Will it continue to grow, and how fast?
* Competition from established players: Will they innovate or maintain their current offerings?
* Regulatory environment: Will there be changes in data protection laws that impact our business model?

### Technical Assumptions

* Can we build a scalable platform with a moderate development budget?
* Which technology stack (e.g., cloud services, databases) is most suitable for our needs?
* How will we ensure seamless integrations with popular accounting software?

### Market Assumptions

* Will freelancers be willing to pay for invoicing software, and how much are they willing to pay?
* Can we reach a significant market share by targeting specific industries or regions?
* What is the average monthly revenue per user (ARPU), and how will it change over time?
